GET YOKED: Extreme Bodybuilding

Download Now

GET YOKED is a Roguelike , Deckbuilder game about becoming an ABSOLUTE UNIT .

TIP: The Windows version has shadows, and the models look better.

WISHLIST ON STEAM

https://store.steampowered.com/app/3594610/

GAMEPLAY

Grow, and sculpt your body to win competitions. Reach the minimum gains required to win.

  • Play workout cards to get Gains , e.g., play Arms to grow those guns.
  • Don't push too hard — too much Fatigue can lead to Injury .
  • Play rest and supplement cards to increase your Gains or decrease muscle Fatigue .
  • Really into your Glutes? No problem become a Glutes purist and get those buns hun.

The journey to Gains -Town is difficult, make smart decisions on your journey to become an ABSOLUTE UNIT .

CONTROLS

Use the mouse to click or drag and drop cards, and press buttons.

TEAM

Game by Greg Austin

Music, Voices & Sound by Sybersyn

THIS IS A PROTOTYPE!

This is an early prototype exploring the potential for GET YOKED to be a full game!

The dream version of this game has:

  • Different starter bodybuilders with unique strengths and weaknesses, and different starter decks.
  • A way to choose your path, with events that give you cards, or different perks.
  • More cards and build paths to make each run more interesting, e.g., Routine cards like Push/Pull so if you play Chest/Back after each other you get more gains.
  • A perk system for more build variety.

I've spent the past two months building this, and while I'm excited about it, it's still very early, so expect some rough edges, but I hope you enjoy what's been created so far!
