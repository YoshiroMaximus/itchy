Auto Necrochess

Download Now

(To donate, just buy the game down there. You can also download it for free!)


After struggling to live with no job, a girl becomes a necromancer and tries to overthrow the King.

________________________________________________________________

A mixture of auto battler and strategy game, this is a project that i started for last year's halloween! Damn, i think i am not good at setting deadlines for myself!

Please report any bugs, if seen! It shouldn't have any, but who knows.

________________________________________________________________

CONTROLS: (though the game is designed to be played entirely with a mouse)

R- Roll

SPACEBAR - Attack / Pause Battle

S (or click and hold thing)- Sell chosen  thing.

F- Go to fullscreen (only on downloaded version)

You can also FREEZE something by clicking on it twice!

NOTES:

-Books need to be at the right of the lane to be able to be used.

-Merging units is important!

-The king gets slowly stronger, so try to rush him or go slow and become strong enough!

-Windows defender might say it's a virus. Sorry, it's a bad side effect of using this really old version of gamemaker. If you don't trust me, just play the HTML5 version! <3

STEPHAUN 41 days ago (+2)

Strategies to try for different row compositions:

Summon units:
Bards, Rockers, and then any combination of Ratling / Summoner / Flying Skulls. Give your skulls a funny bone. Summon unit builds are the strongest in this game.

Bulldozer builds:
Using a Pigman, fully-upgraded Excalibur or Sticky, or a Big Boned- buff them up with everything possible. Shields, milk, milkmen, nurses, farmers and blacksmiths. A single juiced up unit can wipe out entire rows and take on the king 1-on-1 if you buff them up enough.

Viking train:
Viking in front! Assassin behind viking! Nurse behind Assassin! Upgrade them all and they will tear through everything. Bonus points for milkman in front or a stoner / kobold behind. Viking benefits from farmer.

And the most important GENERAL tip of them all:
FULLY UPGRADE UNITS!
Early game especially! So easy to get a fully upgraded stoner, skeleton, viking, milkman or flying skull! And these can carry you for the rest of the run! More importantly they will immediately allow you access to another higher-power unit right off the bat that can totally change and inform how you approach the rest of the round.
