GIMMIKO CLASSIC

WISHLIST THE UPCOMING FULL GAME ON STEAM!!

Defend your shrine against waves of Yokai using magic Gimmick dice in this wacky folklore roguelike!

CONTROLS
WASD: move
Mouse: aim
LMB: Throw dice
RMB: Attract dice

CREDITS
